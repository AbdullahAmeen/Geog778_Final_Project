Right of Way (ROW) And Colocation (COLO) Workflow



Currently I am working with TDS (Telephone and Data System Inc.) as the Right of Way intern. Right of Way has a big metrics which has hundreds of rows and many columns. They use the mentioned metrics to track and monitor the departmental workflow on weekly and monthly basis. Since I was in the last semester of my grad school, so together with my work supervisor I decided to make a web map from the ROW's metrics to communicate the workflow in a more visual and interactive way.

Below web map was designed as my final project when I was a graduate student of Cartography and GIS at the university of Wisconsin Madison.
